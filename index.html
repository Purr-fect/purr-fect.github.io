Ailouros

A cute 3D game by the Purr-fect team

What is this game?

Ailouros is a kind of MOBA, inspired by successful games like Brawl Stars and Rocket League. Its primary game mode will be 3v3 cats.

What are your plans?

Currently, the best way to know what the game will be like is to look at the specifications.

Why are you creating this amazing game?

We're first-year EPITA students, and we were tasked to create some game for our second semester.
