Ailouros

A cute 3D game by the Purr-fect team

What is this game?

Ailouros is a kind of MOBA, inspired by successful games like Brawl Stars and Rocket League. Its primary game mode will be 3v3 cats. Currently, the best way to know what the game will be like is to look at the specifications.

What's the project's timeline?

  • 28/01/2022: Book of specifications accepted
  • 08/03/2022: First defense
  • 25/04/2022: Second defense
  • 06/06/2022 - 17/06/2022: Final defense

Note all documents on the game (specifications, reports) are available on the "downloads" page

Did y'all overcome some big issues?

For the first defense, a member was unavailable for personal reasons. We also had to go through all the documentation of our multiplayer library to understand what we were doing (Mirror).

Why are you creating this amazing game?

We're first-year EPITA students, and we were tasked to create some game for our second semester.
